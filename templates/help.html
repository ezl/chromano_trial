Creating PriceWatches

stock -- type the ticker symbol for the stock you are interested in watching. The adjacent price field should update with the current price of the stock.

high -- decide on a price above the current price of the stock. you will receive notification if the stock trades that level

low -- decide on a price below the current price of the stock. you will receive noticication if the stock trades that level.

if there are multiple price levels to the upside or downside that you are interested in for a given stock, you may create multiple PriceWatches for the same symbol.

high and low boundaries are optional. you may set both, one, or none.

Understanding the PriceWatch bar

Active --a green or red circle indicates whether the watch is active. if it is green, the watch is being monitored by quotesentinel. if it is red, you will not receive any notifications for this watch. all newly created pricewatches are active by default.

Price -- each bar displays the current price of the security

Lower/Upper -- these are thresholds you defined. if the stock touches one of these values, you will receive a notification from quotesentinel for whichever alerts you've set).

SMS alerts -- receive an SMS alert whenever this pricewatch is triggered. if the cell phone icon is on, it will send a text message to the verified cell phone contact number in your profile. you must enter and verify your cell phone number in order to receive messages.

email alerts -- receive an email alert whenever this pricewatch is triggered. if the email icon is on, quotesentinel will send a text message to your alert email address on file. this can be different from your login email if you choose.

Delete Button -- delete any pricewatch by clicking the X on the far right of each price bar.

Sorting PriceWatches

there are many ways to sort your pricewatches. you may manually click and drag any price bar to a new location or you can click on the sort buttons in the header to sort by symbol, current stock price, lower threshold, or upper threshold. quotesentinel will remember your preferences between sessions.
