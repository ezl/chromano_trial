What is QUOTE Sentinel?

QUOTE Sentinel is a web based stock monitoring application that watches your stocks and notifies you whenever your stocks trade to critical prices – that way you can focus on what you do best onstead of having to constantly refresh all your stocks.

What does QUOTE Sentinel cost?

Why do customers love QUOTE Sentinel?

QUOTE Sentinel helps improve your productivity – As an investor, you want to know when the market is actually moving; most of the time its doing nothing. The old solution for most people was to obsessively recheck stock quotes, dividing your attention and wasting time even though nothing was happening.

QUOTE Sentinel reliably sends push notifications so you'll be notified immediately. You won't have to continuously refresh your prices and you can spend more time doing stock research.
